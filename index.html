About

I'am Marufa Sultana

In pursuit of academic excellence, I embarked on my educational journey at the University of Chittagong Hathazari, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering. Graduating in September 2023 with a commendable CGPA of 3.72/4.00, securing a position in the top tier of my class as 5th, I cultivated a comprehensive understanding of computer science fundamentals. During this period, my academic prowess was complemented by a profound interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning.

Driven by a curiosity to delve deeper into the realms of NLP, I am currently enrolled in the Master of Science in Computer Science and Engineering program at the same esteemed institution. My academic pursuits are anchored in a passion for exploring advanced concepts and pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the field. This journey is not merely about acquiring knowledge but about contributing to the evolving landscape of technology.

During my undergraduate years, my academic exploration culminated in a notable achievement – the completion of my thesis titled "Using Ensemble of Transformers for Bragging Detection in Social Media." Under the guidance of Advisor Abu Nowshed Chy, this project not only showcased my ability to apply theoretical concepts but also demonstrated my commitment to addressing real-world challenges. The experience of working on this thesis not only honed my research skills but also fueled my determination to make meaningful contributions to the field of NLP. As I progress in my academic and professional journey, I am excited about the prospect of translating this passion and experience into impactful contributions to the world of computer science.
